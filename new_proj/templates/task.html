Trees are Nature bounty. Trees are of many different kinds. There are flowering trees, which bear blossoms, and non-flowering ones, which do not bloom into flowers. There are evergreen trees, which stay green through the year. There are also deciduous ones which may shed their leaves during a particular season annually making their branches turn bare. Trees make landscapes beautiful. Trees are invaluable to man and terrestrial life forms. Trees maintain ecological balance and equilibrium
